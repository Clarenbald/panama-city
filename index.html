My Family

My family and I used to do Taekwondo for ATA, we went to Panama City, Florida in order to participate in a tournament. We spent a lot of time there, playing on the beach and just enjoying the attractions in general. Depicted to the left is my older brother(Green), my little sister(Pink), and myself(Blue). I don't really remember much of that night but I can say that it was a lot of fun.

My experiences

Some of the things we did included: Go to the tournament, watch some adults practice their moves, then we went back to the hotel and got some of our stuff before heading down to the beach for a beach party under the stars. However my favorite thing was watching my brother practice his Taekwondo. I liked to watch him because I looked up to him and he was much better than me at all of this.
